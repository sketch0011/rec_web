Radharaman Engineering College is one of the Top colleges in Bhopal. It's Affiliated with the University of Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidhyalaya. Get Courses, Facilities, Placements, Recognitions & Accreditations details.

Radharaman Engineering College was founded in 2006 by Tapasya Shiksha Samiti (TSS) with a comprehensive and holistic vision to provide aspiring minds with an arena for their concepts and dreams to manifest and be crowned to shape them. For more than a decade and a half, REC has nurtured minds with a rich legacy of academic excellence to advance the learning pattern of scientific intuition with the concept of futuristic evolution.

Accreditations and Ranking

The pride of technical institutions of Bhopal, Radharaman Institute of Technology and Sciences (RITS) has been accorded accreditation by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi. This prestigious accreditation has put Radharaman Institute of Technology and Science in line with the select few institutes of the country. NBA accredits an institution based on the quality of infrastructure, quality of faculty, quality of teaching learning process and number of placements besides many other parameters. Accreditation is a certification by NBA (AICTE) that the institute is as per the quality norms of AICTE and better than others. Group director Dr. J.L. Rana informed that all the core UG programs of the institute, namely, computer science and engineering, electronics and communication engineering, electrical and electronics engineering and mechanical engineering have been accredited for three years up to 14th March 2015.

Infrastructure and Facilities

Library

The libraries of the Radharaman Group of Institutes are important for students and faculty. All individual colleges have their own libraries with a large collection of relevant books and journals. The library's total book collection includes over 27,620 in Rit, 14,484 in Rec, 11,072 in Law, 4,276 in RCP, and 2,980 in Tears.
Libraries provide services to students, staff, and faculty to keep their knowledge up to date and to support research excellence through innovation and creativity in teaching/learning activities.

Seminar Hall

Each faculty has its own amphitheater-style seminar hall, acoustically designed and adequately equipped to accommodate 120 students, as well as projection equipment and internet access. An air-conditioned common seminar hall with 350 seats and several air-conditioned conferences and discussion rooms equipped with all modern audiovisual equipment.

Computer Lab

Radhraman Institute of Technology & The science campus has 1,500, computers of the latest configuration connected to a single LAN. The above computer systems together with the corresponding number of printers installed in more than 50 different laboratories and offices and server rooms (with five-blade servers) are part of the RGI system infrastructure. Each laboratory has all the necessary (licensed) software for the intended laboratory work. RGI has a Microsoft Campus Agreement that allows RGI to use all Microsoft software products.
